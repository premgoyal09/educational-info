About

Mussoorie International School (MIS), established in 1984, is an all-girls residential school in Mussoorie, Uttarakhand, India. Known for its beautiful Himalayan setting, MIS offers a blend of academics and holistic development. It provides a rigorous curriculum with a focus on critical thinking, preparing students for the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE), Cambridge International Examinations, and the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IBDP). Beyond academics, MIS emphasizes extracurricular activities, fostering well-rounded individuals with strong leadership and teamwork skills.
